About

AnimePop is a wiki site where you can view the popular animes all through out. A wiki ( i/ˈwɪki/ WIK-ee) is a website that provides collaborative modification of its content and structure directly from the web browser.

This site recommends you the most popular anime. It also will provide you the synopsis or summary of the anime you have choosen to view so you can understand the story of the anime.
